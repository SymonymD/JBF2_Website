Version 3.0, release date August 31, 1999

Welcome to Free Solitaire v3.0!

Free Solitaire is a completely free collection of solitaire games. Free Solitaire
is advertiser-supported software and includes an advertisement engine that will
display ads in each game window during play. To remove the ads, and to receive more
features, please consider upgrading to the shareware Solitaire Plus! for a modest
fee.  See Solitaire Plus at http://www.solitaireplus.com. Also, for 300 solitaire
games see Pretty Good Solitaire 99 at http://www.goodsol.com.

Free Solitaire includes the great features of past versions including:

** The original 8 games: Forty Thieves, FreeCell, Indian Patience, Klondike,
   Montana, Pyramid, Sea Towers, and Spider
** Two selectable card sizes 
** Unlimited undo of moves 
** Use of any BMP, JPEG, or PCX image as a card back
   (requires high-color or better)

The new features included in Free Solitaire v3.0 are:

** Two additional games, Golf and Double Klondike (10 games total) 
** Smart dragging - cards need only be nudged to move them to a target pile 
** Much improved on-line help 
** Sound effects 
** Supports 256-color displays (high color is still recommended)
