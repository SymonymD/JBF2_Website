This site is a classic example of the HOME OFFICE CURSE: When you don't have Dilbert's Boss hanging over your shoulder you have a nasty tendency to spend more time playing with your toys than doing honest work. Consequently, I have divided the site into two sections.

In the Professional Area you will find information on my interests as a Real Estate Attorney, my Resumé and References, general legal resources and specific information on Connecticut Law and Real Estate. There's even a Comparative Loan Calculator for when you have to refinance the house to buy that new computer. This side of the site also has a collection of quotes on the subject of Free Speech and the First Amendment. Eventually I will be adding a detailed Manual on the sale and purchase of Residential Real Estate in Connecticut. And, last but not least, the Top Ten Reasons Why Real Estate Lawyers Get Ulcers.

In the Personal Area you will find links to Marxism, great flicks, bad jokes, interesting people, a dapper but humourless detective, terrific (if somewhat lethal) food including the Best Chili Hot Dog Recipe in the known universe, esoteric brews, lots of information on the State of Connecticut (including the infamous speed trap industry), a plethora of vocabulary builders, the entire US Space Program, a collection of Search Engines, the sexiest woman who ever lived and the Cheshire Cat. You will also discover what S.O.S. really stands for and why Rudyard Kipling was not popular at Bridal Showers.

Down below on the main page you'll find a link to the only viable Presidential Campaign for sane voters, the Goddard Space Flight Center's Astronomy Picture of the Day to expand your horizons, and some link generators that are entertaining, educational or simply appalling. (I have no control over where those things take you so use them with caution if you are under age, easily shocked or Bob Dole.) Be sure to check out the fine folks at GeoCities who make all this possible (or whose fault it is, depending on your point of view).

So jump on in and wander around a bit. This is a work in progress (it would've progressed farther if ferschlugginer Netscape hadn't eaten my Bookmark file) so be careful walking under ladders and try not to leave footprints in the cement. Pick up a door prize in the Free Graphics section, don't forget to leave your pithy comments for posterity in the Guest Book and please come again.
