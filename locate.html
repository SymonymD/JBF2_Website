DIRECTIONS

12 Washington Circle
Unionville, Ct., 06085

FROM EAST AND NORTH:

Take Route I-84 Westbound to Route 4 in Farmington (The UCONN Medical Center Exit) and go West on Route 4 through Farmington Center to Unionville Center, about 4 miles. In the Center take a left and go South on Route 177 (Plainville Avenue). Go over the iron bridge on the Farmington River and then up the hill to the second traffic light after the bridge. Take a right onto Burlington Road (note the scenic abandoned gas station on the corner) and go 1 mile to the first stop sign (You will cross the Town line into Burlington just before the stop sign.) See below.

FROM WEST AND SOUTH:

Take Route I-84 Eastbound to Route 72 Westbound in Plainville (this is a left exit) and go West on Route 72 to the first exit, Washington Street (Route 177). Go right off the exit and North on Route 177 to Route 6 (Tunxis Community College is on the right at the intersection). Cross Route 6 and continue North on Route 177 to the 4th traffic light (about 4 miles). Take a left onto Burlington Road (noting the above referenced scenic abandoned gas station) and go one mile to the first stop sign.

FROM THE STOP SIGN ON BURLINGTON ROAD:

This is a 5 way intersection. Take a sharp left onto Washington Street (this is the only road that goes down hill). Go to the bottom of the hill and cross West Side Boulevard into the smaller road with the "No Outlet" sign. This is still Washington Street which becomes Washington Circle when it crosses back into Farmington. We are the last house on the left at the end, a brown Cape Cod. Don't run over the cat.

When you get lost call for help (675-8489) and we'll come get you.
There's a pay phone at the abandoned gas station on Plainville Avenue.
